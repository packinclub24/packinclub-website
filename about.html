Our Story • Founded 2024, Delhi

Every shift starts with a moment of clarity.
Ours arrived twice.

Packin Club is more than a packaging company. We exist to help businesses leave the single-use plastic era behind, without giving up the strength, durability, or trust their customers expect.

The question that started it

"Can packaging be genuinely compostable, without compromising on quality?"

Two moments
01

When India announced its single-use plastic ban, it landed as more than policy for our founder, Vikas Jha. Coming from a second-generation plastic-film trading family, he saw the disruption and the opening in the same instant.

02

Around the same time, a coffee with a friend visiting from Spain laid bare an uncomfortable truth: many celebrated "eco-friendly" alternatives, paper straws among them, simply fall apart in real-world use. Good intentions weren't enough. The products had to actually work.

Founded in 2024 in Delhi, Packin Club became the answer to that question, a catalyst for businesses ready to lead the shift to a plastic-free future.

A message from our founder
"When I started Packin Club, the goal was never only to protect the planet, it was to protect people. Plastic shows up everywhere we look now: in our food, our water, even our bloodstream. I couldn't accept that convenience should cost us our health or the next generation's future.

So we set out to make packaging that's safe, strong, and genuinely sustainable, for businesses, for families, and for what comes after us. In the end, this work isn't measured by what we make. It's measured by what we leave behind."
Vikas Jha
